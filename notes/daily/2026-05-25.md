## Core Takeaways

- [ ] **AA is not about "no gas" — it's about programmable control.** Instead of "has private key / doesn't have private key," you get "allowed to do X under conditions Y, for duration Z, up to amount W."
- [ ] **Session Key is the foundation for Agent Wallets.** You don't want to give an Agent your main private key, and you don't want it interrupting you for every signature. Session Key = temporary, scoped, revocable permission token.
- [ ] **AA doesn't make AI freer — it wraps AI's freedom in rules.** The clearer the policy (what contracts, what amount, how long, who can revoke), the harder the safety boundary.
